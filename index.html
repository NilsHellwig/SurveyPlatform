Thank you for taking the time to complete our study. For this study, students of Information Science / Media Informatics receive 0.5 VP upon completion. As part of the seminar 'Informationsverhalten verstehen' by Dr. David Elsweiler, we are conducting an online study on the credibility of websites. You will look at a total of 4 screenshots of websites. After you have looked at them, you will have to answer questions about them. Please do not use any external sources while conducting the study and please conduct this study on a computer and not on a mobile device. Furthermore, please stay on this page and do not close the browser window until you have completed the study.
